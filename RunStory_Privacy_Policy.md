# Privacy Policy for RunStory

**Effective Date**: December 28, 2025  
**Last Updated**: December 28, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to RunStory ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application RunStory (the "App").

By using RunStory, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you use RunStory, we may collect the following information:

- **Account Information**: When you connect your Strava or Garmin account, we receive:
  - User ID
  - Username
  - Email address (if provided by the service)
  - Profile information

- **Activity Data**: We access your running/fitness activity data from Strava or Garmin, including:
  - Activity name, date, and time
  - Distance, duration, and pace
  - Route/GPS coordinates (polyline data)
  - Elevation gain
  - Device information (watch/tracker model)
  - Activity type (run, walk, cycling, etc.)

- **Photos**: If you choose to add custom background images to your overlays:
  - Photos from your device's gallery
  - Photos taken with your device's camera

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: 
  - Device type and model
  - Operating system version
  - App version
  - Device identifiers

- **Usage Data**:
  - Features used within the app
  - Templates selected
  - Crash reports and error logs

### 1.3 Location Information

We access location data only through your Strava/Garmin activities to display your running routes on the map. We do not track your real-time location.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Core Functionality**: To display your activity data and create visual overlays
- **Authentication**: To verify your identity via Strava/Garmin OAuth
- **Personalization**: To remember your preferences and settings
- **Improvement**: To analyze app usage and improve features
- **Support**: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Where We Store Your Data

- **Local Storage**: Your access tokens and user preferences are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage (Flutter Secure Storage)
- **Backend Server**: Activity data is temporarily cached on our servers (hosted on Vercel) to improve performance
- **Third-Party Services**: We rely on Strava and Garmin APIs to access your activity data

### 3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption**: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Secure Storage**: Access tokens are stored using platform-specific secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android)
- **Limited Access**: We only request the minimum permissions necessary for app functionality
- **No Password Storage**: We never store your Strava or Garmin passwords

### 3.3 Data Retention

- **Activity Data**: Cached activity data is retained for 30 days or until you log out
- **Access Tokens**: Stored until you revoke access or log out
- **Generated Images**: Saved overlays are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to our servers
